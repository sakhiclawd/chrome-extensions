# Briefly AI: AI Webpage Summary - Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 13, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Briefly AI: AI Webpage Summary" Chrome extension ("the Extension") handles your information. Your privacy is important to us.

## 1. Information We Collect

The Extension is designed to be privacy-conscious and collects minimal information necessary for its function:

*   **Webpage Content:** When you activate the Extension on a webpage, it extracts the visible text content of that active tab. This content is temporarily processed solely for the purpose of generating a summary.
*   **No Personal Identifiable Information (PII):** The Extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, browsing history, IP address, or unique identifiers) from your device.

## 2. How We Use Information

The extracted webpage content is used exclusively for the following purpose:

*   **Summarization:** The text content is sent to a third-party Artificial Intelligence (AI) service (the "AI Backend") for real-time summarization. The AI Backend processes this content to generate a concise summary which is then displayed to you.

## 3. Data Storage and Retention

*   **Temporary Processing:** Webpage content sent to the AI Backend is processed in real-time and is NOT permanently stored by the Extension or the AI Backend after the summary has been generated and delivered to you.
*   **No Local Storage:** The Extension does not store any webpage content or summary data on your local device beyond what is necessary for immediate display within the pop-up.

## 4. Third-Party AI Backend

*   The Extension relies on a third-party AI service to perform summarization. While we select reputable services, we cannot control the privacy practices of these external services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any AI Backend you choose to integrate with the Extension (as configured in `background.js` by the user).
*   The default configuration of the Extension will attempt to use a publicly available AI endpoint, which may have its own terms and privacy policy.

## 5. Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. While no online service can guarantee absolute security, we implement reasonable measures designed to protect the information that is temporarily transmitted for summarization.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the Extension's distribution channels or by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the Extension's data practices, please refer to the support channels provided by the Extension's publisher.
