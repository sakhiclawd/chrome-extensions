# Privacy Policy: Briefly AI

**Last Updated: March 15, 2026**

## 1. Overview
Briefly AI ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed to provide webpage summaries using AI.

## 2. Data Collection
- **Personal Information:** Briefly AI does not collect any personal identification information (PII) like names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
- **Browsing History:** We do not track your browsing history. The extension only accesses the content of the current tab when you explicitly trigger a summarization action.

## 3. Data Processing
When you request a summary, the text of the webpage is sent to a secure AI processing endpoint to generate the summary. This data is processed in real-time and is not stored or used for training purposes.

## 4. Permissions
- `activeTab`: Used to access the content of the page you want to summarize.
- `scripting`: Used to extract text for processing.
- `storage`: Used to save your local extension settings.

## 5. Third Parties
We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to outside parties.
