# Briefly Analyzer - Chrome Web Store Description

## Tagline
Instantly analyze competitor traffic, SEO, and backlinks for any website.

## Summary
Briefly Analyzer provides a high-level SEO and marketing snapshot for any domain directly in your browser. Whether you're researching competitors or auditing your own performance, get real-time data on traffic volume, organic visibility, and backlink strength in seconds.

## Key Features
- **Domain Snapshot**: Quickly view traffic estimates and engagement metrics.
- **SEO Insights**: See ranking keyword counts and estimated organic search cost.
- **Authority Profile**: Audit the total number of backlinks and referring domains.
- **Smart Observation Engine**: Get factual, automated insights about a domain's performance.
- **PMLibrary Integration**: Save analysis snapshots to your library for historical tracking and comparison.

## How to Use
1. Navigate to any website you want to analyze.
2. Click the Briefly Analyzer icon in your toolbar.
3. Toggle between **Root Domain** and **Subdomain** scope.
4. Browse through the **Traffic**, **Keywords**, and **Backlinks** tabs.
5. Click **Save Snapshot** to archive the report to your PMLibrary.

## Privacy Policy
Briefly Analyzer respects your privacy. It only analyzes the domain of the active tab when you explicitly open the extension. No browsing history is tracked or stored outside of the reports you choose to save.
